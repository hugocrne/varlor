# Product Requirements Document - varlor

**Author:** Hugo
**Date:** 2025-12-05

## Step 2: Project Discovery

### 2.1 Target Audience

**Primary Target Users:**
- Players looking for a faith-based alternative to mainstream MMORPGs
- Christian gamers seeking values-aligned entertainment
- Parents seeking safe gaming environments for their children
- Gaming communities interested in historical/mythological settings

**Secondary Target Users:**
- MMORPG enthusiasts exploring new themes
- Players interested in cooperative rather than competitive gameplay
- Users seeking games with strong moral foundations

### 2.2 Market Analysis

**Current Market Gap:**
- Most MMORPGs focus on combat, mythology from non-Christian traditions, or fantasy settings
- Limited options for faith-based gaming in the MMORPG space
- Growing demand for value-aligned entertainment options

**Competitive Landscape:**
- Traditional MMORPGs (World of Warcraft, Final Fantasy XIV) - high production value but secular themes
- Christian games (often smaller scale, limited MMORPG options)
- Educational/historical games - limited multiplayer functionality

### 2.3 User Needs & Pain Points

**Primary Needs:**
1. Engaging gameplay that aligns with Christian values
2. Community building without toxic gaming environments
3. Opportunity to explore biblical history and teachings interactively
4. Family-friendly multiplayer gaming experience

**Pain Points with Current Options:**
- Secular games often conflict with Christian values
- Limited multiplayer options in faith-based games
- Lack of high-production Christian gaming content
- Difficulty finding wholesome gaming communities

### 2.4 Value Proposition

**Core Value:**
"Experience the epic stories of faith through immersive, cooperative gameplay in a beautifully crafted world that brings biblical history to life."

**Key Differentiators:**
- First major MMORPG built on Christian foundations
- Focus on cooperation, service, and spiritual growth
- High-quality production values with faith-aligned content
- Safe, moderated community environment

### 2.5 Success Metrics

**Engagement Metrics:**
- Daily Active Users (DAU)
- Average session duration
- Community participation (events, group activities)

**Impact Metrics:**
- User feedback on spiritual/growth aspects
- Community health indicators (low toxicity, high cooperation)
- Retention rates beyond typical MMORPG benchmarks

**Business Metrics:**
- Conversion rates (free to premium)
- Community growth
- Partnership opportunities with faith-based organizations
