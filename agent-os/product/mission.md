# Product Mission

## Pitch
Varlor is a universal data intelligence platform that helps European enterprises gain complete control over their data by providing automated ingestion, intelligent cleaning, ontological modeling, and AI-powered analysis—transforming raw data chaos into precise, actionable decisions.

## Users

### Primary Customers
- **SMEs (Small & Medium Enterprises)**: Organizations with 50-500 employees lacking dedicated data science teams but drowning in heterogeneous data sources
- **Mid-Market Companies (ETI)**: 500-5000 employees with complex data landscapes requiring unified analysis across departments and legacy systems
- **Enterprise Accounts**: Large corporations needing sovereign, air-gapped data intelligence solutions with military-grade security and compliance

### User Personas

**Data Manager** (35-50 years)
- **Role:** Head of Data / IT Director
- **Context:** Manages data infrastructure for a manufacturing company with 15+ years of accumulated data across ERP, CRM, IoT sensors, and Excel files
- **Pain Points:** Spends 70% of time locating, cleaning, and reconciling data instead of analyzing it. Cannot answer basic business questions without weeks of manual work. Lacks budget for full data science team.
- **Goals:** Automate data cleaning and analysis. Generate reliable reports for C-suite. Identify operational inefficiencies and cost savings opportunities.

**Operations Analyst** (28-40 years)
- **Role:** Supply Chain Analyst / Operations Manager
- **Context:** Works in logistics/manufacturing, responsible for optimizing processes and reducing waste
- **Pain Points:** Data exists in silos across different systems. Cannot track end-to-end processes. Anomalies and bottlenecks go undetected until they become critical issues.
- **Goals:** Real-time visibility into operations. Automated anomaly detection. Clear insights into root causes of delays and inefficiencies.

**Compliance Officer** (35-55 years)
- **Role:** CISO / Compliance Director
- **Context:** Enterprise organization with strict data sovereignty requirements (government, defense, banking, healthcare)
- **Pain Points:** Cannot use cloud-based analytics due to regulatory constraints. Requires complete audit trails and access controls. Needs air-gapped deployment capabilities.
- **Goals:** Sovereign data solution with zero external dependencies. Complete audit logs. Role-based access control. Deploy in secure, isolated environments.

## The Problem

### Data Exists, But Value Doesn't
European enterprises accumulate vast amounts of data across decades of operations, yet struggle to extract meaningful value. 80% of data teams' time is wasted on data discovery, cleaning, reconciliation, and basic formatting—leaving minimal capacity for actual analysis and decision-making.

**Specific Challenges:**
- **Heterogeneous Sources**: Data trapped in legacy ERPs, modern APIs, Excel files, databases, IoT devices, and proprietary formats
- **Quality Crisis**: Missing values, inconsistent formats, duplicates, contradictory information, and unstructured chaos
- **No Semantic Understanding**: Raw tables without business context—unable to answer "why" or "what if" questions
- **Integration Complexity**: Each new data source requires months of custom engineering
- **Sovereignty Requirements**: European organizations cannot use US-based cloud analytics (GDPR, CLOUD Act, security policies)

**Our Solution:** Varlor provides universal data ingestion (accepts any format, any source), automated quality assessment and cleaning, ontological modeling (transforms raw data into business entities and relationships), AI-powered analysis and insights, and complete sovereignty (self-hosted, air-gap capable, zero external dependencies).

### Vendor Lock-in and Lack of Adaptability
Existing data platforms force organizations to conform to rigid schemas and workflows. When business needs evolve or new data sources emerge, companies face expensive migrations or custom development. Small-to-medium enterprises cannot afford Palantir-level solutions.

**Our Solution:** Varlor is designed for universal adaptability through flexible ontology modeling, configurable analysis axes, extensible connector architecture, and industry-specific template packs. Organizations pay for capabilities they need, not enterprise-only pricing.

## Differentiators

### Universal Ingestion Without Compromise
Unlike traditional BI tools (Tableau, Power BI) that require pre-cleaned, structured data, or modern data platforms (Snowflake, Databricks) that require extensive data engineering, Varlor ingests any format (CSV, Excel, JSON, XML, Parquet, logs, proprietary formats), connects to any source (modern APIs, legacy databases, SAP, industrial systems), and handles any quality level (incomplete, inconsistent, massive, chaotic). This results in weeks instead of months for new data source integration, and zero rejected datasets.

### Ontological Intelligence
Unlike descriptive analytics tools that only visualize data, Varlor constructs a semantic business model—transforming raw tables into meaningful entities (Customer, Order, Machine, Event) and relationships. This ontology enables cross-source analysis without manual joins, AI-powered insights based on business context, and adaptation to any industry vertical. This results in 10x faster insight generation and analysis that answers "why" not just "what."

### Sovereign and Air-Gap Ready
Unlike SaaS analytics platforms (Looker, Mode, Hex) or US-based solutions with external dependencies, Varlor provides completely self-hosted authentication (Keycloak, no Google/Auth0), air-gap deployment capability (no internet required), European-first compliance (GDPR native, no CLOUD Act exposure), and full audit trails for regulated industries. This results in deployment in defense, healthcare, banking, and government sectors where competitors cannot operate.

### Automated Intelligence, Not Just Dashboards
Unlike manual BI tools requiring analysts to build every report, Varlor automatically detects anomalies in data quality and business metrics, generates natural language insights explaining what's happening and why, suggests priority focus areas and investigation paths, and creates versioned, contextualized reports. This results in actionable insights for non-technical users and 90% reduction in manual analysis time.

## Key Features

### Core Features
- **Universal Data Ingestion**: Upload files (CSV, Excel, JSON, XML) or connect to databases, APIs, ERPs, and legacy systems—Varlor automatically detects schemas and handles any quality level
- **Intelligent Data Cleaning**: Automated detection and correction of missing values, type inconsistencies, duplicates, outliers, and format errors with full transparency and audit trails
- **Ontology Modeling**: Visual editor to define business entities (Customer, Product, Order) and relationships—Varlor suggests mappings and unifies heterogeneous data sources into coherent business model
- **Configurable Analysis Axes**: Define custom dimensions (time, geography, customer segment, product category) and metrics to explore data exactly how your business needs
- **AI-Powered Insights**: Natural language summaries explaining patterns, anomalies, correlations, and trends—not just charts, but interpretation and recommended actions

### Collaboration Features
- **Data Catalog**: Centralized inventory of all datasets with quality metrics, lineage tracking, version history, and usage statistics
- **Role-Based Access Control**: Granular permissions by dataset, report type, and action (import, analyze, view)—supporting multi-tenant organizations with strict separation
- **Audit & Compliance**: Complete activity logs of who accessed what data, when, and what changes were made—supporting SOC2, ISO27001, and regulatory requirements

### Advanced Features
- **Automated Report Generation**: Schedule intelligent reports that update automatically, combining visualizations with AI-generated narratives and business recommendations
- **Anomaly Detection**: Real-time monitoring of data quality and business metrics with configurable alerting and root cause analysis
- **Multi-Source Analysis**: Analyze relationships across disparate data sources unified through ontology—no manual data engineering required
- **Self-Hosted Deployment**: Deploy on-premise, private cloud, or air-gapped environments with complete sovereignty and zero external dependencies
